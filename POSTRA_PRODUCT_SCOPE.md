# Postra Product Scope

## MVP Features (Phase 1)

### 1) Chat-Based Control (Core)
Creators can issue simple chat commands and Postra interprets intent and executes actions.

Example commands:
- "Image post kar"
- "Video schedule kar"
- "Comment pe DM bhej"
- "Kal 6 baje post kar"

Flow:
- Chat input → AI intent understanding → action execution

### 2) Content Handling
Supported in MVP:
- ✅ Image upload
- ✅ Video upload (reel/feed)
- ✅ Auto caption generation
- ✅ Auto SEO-friendly title generation
- ✅ Auto hashtag generation
- ✅ Reel cover image selection from an uploaded image

Not in MVP:
- ❌ Video generation
- ❌ Video editing

### 3) Scheduled Posting
- Schedule image posts
- Schedule video/reel posts
- Auto-generate caption + hashtags
- Creator approval before posting (safety gate)

### 4) Comment → DM Automation (Meta-Allowed)
- Trigger DM from comment keywords
- Use approved DM templates only
- Built for Business accounts

Examples:
- Comment: "price" → pricing DM
- Comment: "link" → link DM

### 5) DM Automation (Safe Version)
- Keyword-based DM replies
- Pre-approved templates
- No spammy bulk messaging

Examples:
- "price" → pricing DM
- "collab" → collaboration info DM

## Explicitly Out of MVP (Risky or Out of Scope)
- ❌ Competitor scraping
- ❌ Auto liking / auto following
- ❌ Mass unsolicited DMs
- ❌ Personal account automation
- ❌ Fake engagement tactics

## Later Features (Phase 2 and 3)

### 6) Story Interactions (Allowed Scope)
- Auto reply to story mentions
- Auto reply to story DMs
- Excludes polling/voting automation

### 7) DM Funnels + CTA Flows
- Guided DM conversational flows
- Button-based choices
- Keyword-based routing

Example:
- "Buy" → pricing
- "Demo" → booking link

### 8) Analytics (Value Layer)
Performance dashboard:
- Best-performing posts
- Engagement trends
- Reach and interaction summaries

Smart suggestions:
- Best posting time recommendations
- Audience engagement prompts (e.g., reply to top fans)

Principle:
- Actionable insights only, no fake metrics

### 9) Reports
- Auto-generated PDF reports
- Weekly/monthly summaries
- Exportable reports for creator-brand partnerships

### 10) Lead Capture + CRM Sync
From DMs:
- Lead capture
- Tagging by interest/country
- Follow-up reminders

Integrations:
- Google Sheets
- Airtable
- Notion
- Zapier

### 11) Conversion Tracking (Safe)
- Link click tracking
- Form submission tracking
- Signup/purchase tracking via creator-owned properties

Output:
- Logged and visible in the Postra dashboard

### 12) Product Catalog Sync (Advanced/Paid)
- Product list sync
- Product tagging in posts
- Pricing and availability updates

## Final MVP Summary
Postra MVP includes:
- Chat-based Instagram management
- Image and video posting
- AI-generated captions and hashtags
- Comment-to-DM automation
- Keyword DM flows
- Post scheduling
- Reel cover image support

Postra MVP excludes:
- Video generation
- Video editing
- Shady growth hacks
- Meta policy violations
